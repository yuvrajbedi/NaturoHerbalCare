FAQ

Hair oil questions, answered.

How often should I use the hair oil?

Use one to three times a week depending on scalp dryness and routine. A small amount is enough for a lightweight massage.

Can I leave it overnight?

Yes. You can leave it for at least one hour or overnight, then wash thoroughly with the Ayurvedic Shampoo.

Will it make my hair greasy?

The formula is designed to be lightweight and non-sticky. Start with a small amount, especially if you have fine hair.
