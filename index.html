FAQ

Clear answers before checkout.

Can Ayurvedic shampoo help with hair fall?

Hair fall can have many causes, but a gentle Ayurvedic shampoo can support the routine by cleansing buildup, calming the scalp, and reducing breakage from harsh washing.

How often should I use the shampoo?

Use two to three times a week, or as needed based on scalp oiliness and lifestyle. Follow with the hair oil ritual weekly for nourishment.

Does the hair oil feel sticky?

The herbal hair oil is designed to feel lightweight and non-sticky. Massage a small amount into the scalp and leave it for at least one hour before washing.

Can it help with dandruff?

The shampoo includes anti-fungal and anti-bacterial care cues with neem and scalp-friendly herbs that help reduce visible flakes and buildup.

Hair care tips

Small habits that make the ritual work harder.

Massage the scalp

Use fingertips, not nails, and massage oil for three to five minutes to support circulation.

Wash gently

Focus shampoo on the scalp first, then let the rinse cleanse the lengths without rough scrubbing.

Stay consistent

Natural hair routines work best with steady use across several weeks, not one rushed wash day.
